What's new

Stay safe in the heat

The heat has arrived. Find out where to keep cool and how to protect yourself during hot weather. Check on others frequently.

Free symphony concert at Sunset Beach

Join us for a free open air concert featuring the Vancouver Symphony Orchestra on July 6 from 8pm to 9:30pm at Sunset Beach.

The concert will include classical favourites to film score hits.

Kitsilano concession pop-up patio

Enjoy mountain views and alcohol service at Kitsilano Pool Concession every weekend.

Food is served Wednesday to Sunday, 11am to dusk, and alcohol, Friday to Sunday, noon to 10pm.

Public notices available online

Review notifications on public hearings and tax, by-law, election, and other items regularly. 

Find out what's happening and how and when you can share your views on items that go to Council. 

Share accessibility barriers

We want to hear from anyone in Vancouver who:

  • Experiences a barrier
  • Witnesses someone experiencing a barrier

Your feedback will help us understand the barriers people face to remove them.
